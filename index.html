Frequently Asked Questions

Are safety nets to be installed taut?

Safety nets for catching people and debris are to have a slight sag when installed. This is for deflection. Therefore normally 5% is added to your measurements to achieve this sag. Platform Nets for people to walk on are to be taut, no % added to the size is required.

Are your nets custom made?

Yes, our nets are custom made to fit your specific needs and dimensions.
